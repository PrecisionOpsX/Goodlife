About Goodlife Physical Medicine

Welcome to the Goodlife

Goodlife Physical Medicine delivers integrated, patient-focused care through a team of specialists in chiropractic, pain management, physical medicine, and conventional medicine. Using an integrative approach, the team combines traditional medical care with complementary therapies to create customized treatment plans.

As a multidisciplinary one-stop clinic, Goodlife offers on-site diagnostic imaging, including digital X-rays, ultrasound, and MRI, allowing for fast and accurate diagnoses. The focus is on long-term relief and overall wellness without relying on addictive medications, unnecessary surgery, or invasive procedures.

At Goodlife Physical Medicine, we believe that effective healing starts with a personalized approach, which is why our chiropractors are highly trained in a diverse spectrum of adjustment techniques. We recognize that every body is unique; what works for an athlete seeking a robust, high-velocity adjustment may not be suitable for a patient managing chronic sensitivity or recovering from an injury.

Our practitioners are skilled in everything from gentle, low-force mobilizations - perfect for those who prefer a subtler touch to traditional, more assertive manual adjustments for those who respond best to deeper structural realignment. By prioritizing your specific comfort level and clinical needs, we ensure that your treatment plan is never "one-size-fits-all", but rather a tailored experience designed to help you achieve optimal wellness at your own pace.

Meet the Owners:

Adam Weitzman, MD

Medical Director

Dr. Weitzman focuses on state-of-the-art therapies for acute and chronic pain, including interventional pain management. He leads a multidisciplinary approach that integrates chiropractic, physical therapy, and massage therapy to address the root cause of pain.

Dave Carry, DC

Chiropractor

Dr. Carry is dedicated to modern, evidence-based chiropractic care for acute and chronic pain. His expertise includes spinal adjustments and a multidisciplinary approach that combines corrective exercises, soft-tissue therapy, and rehabilitative care.

How the Goodlife Started:

"I became a pain management doctor because I could see firsthand how pain ruins so many lives and I wanted to help. But I quickly learned that pain won't respond to just one thing. Sometimes, the body just says "NO!" To really make a difference, many specialties and techniques are needed together, in the right order and with the right timing. But coodinating all of that is hard and doesn't really exist anywhere!... Until now."

Dr. Adam Weitzman

"I believe in whole-body wellness and have halped thousands of patients improve their everyday lives through chiropractic. However, I had patients that needed more than chiropractic could offer and I am the kind of guy who wants to be able to help everyone. I needed a Team!"

Dr. Dave Carry

"Together, we founded Goodlife to give every patient the best chance at complete recovery, and a gain a clear, comprehensive viewpoint of their condition. By creating a space where all of our patients' treatments can be performed under one convenient roof, we're targeting the root of the issue for each patient instead of simply treating their symptoms. This takes a high level of coordination to pull off. So every practitioner on our Goodlife team knows every patient's customized wellness plan. It's through keen attention to progress that we're able to see each patient through each step of their wellness plan. Working as a team, we're confident we have all the right tools to get our patients back to the Goodlife, and out of pain for good."
