Manipulation Under Anesthesia (MUA)

What is Manipulation Under Anesthesia (MUA)?

Manipulation Under Anesthesia (MUA) is a gentle, carefully guided procedure designed for patients who haven't found lasting relief through traditional conservative care alone. Performed in a controlled medical setting, MUA allows the body to fully relax so restricted joints and tight tissues can be safely mobilized without the resistance or discomfort often felt during regular treatments. Many patients feel nervous at first, which is completely normal-but most are surprised by how smooth the experience is and how quickly they notice improved mobility, reduced pain, and better overall function afterward. MUA is not surgery, does not involve cutting or incisions, and is intended to help restore movement so your body can heal and respond better to ongoing care.

At Goodlife, your comfort, safety, and confidence are our top priorities. Our experienced team takes the time to thoroughly evaluate your condition, explain every step of the process, and determine whether MUA is the right option for you. You're never rushed or pressured-just supported with honest answers and personalized care. If you've been struggling with chronic pain or limited movement and feel unsure about your next step, we invite you to book a consultation to learn more about MUA and how it may help you move forward with relief and confidence.

How It Works

Right for You?

MUA could be right for you if you experience any of the following:

  • Persistent pain that hasn't improved with chiropractic care or PT.
  • Chronic back, neck, or joint pain
  • Limited range of motion or stiffness
  • Pain from old injuries or post-surgical scar tissue
  • Frozen shoulder or joint adhesions

Potential Benefits

Pevious patients who recieved MUA have seen:

  • May help avoid surgery
  • Breaks up scar tissue and adhesions
  • Restores mobility and joint function
  • Reduces chronic pain and stiffness
  • Accelerates progress with rehabilitation
